Privacy Policy

Hampter Login  ·  Developed by Hampter Games  ·  Last updated: May 2026

Hampter Login is a Chrome extension that automates the login process for Atlantis Education. It does this by reading a magic link email from your Gmail inbox and navigating to it on your behalf. This policy explains exactly what is accessed, how it is used, and what is never collected.

What data is accessed

With your explicit permission, Hampter Login accesses your Gmail inbox using Google's Gmail API with the gmail.readonly scope. This access is used solely to locate a login email sent by Atlantis Education and extract the magic link URL it contains.

The only other piece of data the extension stores is your email address, which you voluntarily enter into the extension's settings popup. This is saved locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local).

What data is never collected or transmitted

Hampter Login does not:

  • Transmit your email address or any Gmail content to any external server
  • Store any data outside of your own device
  • Read any emails other than the specific login email from Atlantis Education
  • Track, log, or analyse your usage in any way
  • Share any data with third parties under any circumstances

How Gmail access works

All Gmail API requests are made directly from Chrome's extension service worker to Google's servers, using an OAuth 2.0 token that Chrome manages on your behalf. No external server, proxy, or third-party service is involved at any point. Your Gmail content never leaves your device except in the direct, encrypted connection between Chrome and Google.

Permissions explained

Hampter Login requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • identity — allows Chrome to manage the Google OAuth login flow and provide an access token to the extension. You will see a standard Google consent screen the first time you use the extension.
  • storage — allows the extension to save your email address locally in your browser so you don't have to re-enter it.
  • gmail.readonly — allows the extension to search your inbox for the Atlantis login email and read its contents to extract the magic link URL. The extension cannot send, delete, or modify emails.

Data retention

The only data retained is your email address in chrome.storage.local, which stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension or clearing its storage via Chrome's developer tools. No data is held anywhere else.

Children's privacy

Hampter Login does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone. As no data is collected or stored externally, this extension is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated in a future version of the extension, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect the change. The latest version will always be available at hamptergamesofficial.github.io/AtlantisAutoLogin/privacy.html . Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
