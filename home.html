How it works

Five steps, zero effort

Step 01

🐹

Click the button

An orange Log in with Hampter button appears below the Atlantis login button automatically.

Step 02

✉️

Form submitted

The extension fills in your email address and submits the Atlantis login form on your behalf.

Step 03

🔐

Gmail access granted

Chrome asks for one-time permission to read your Gmail. Click Allow — this only happens once.

Step 04

📬

Magic link found

The extension watches your inbox and locates the login email from Atlantis as soon as it arrives.

Step 05

✅

You're in

The magic link is followed automatically. You're logged in before you've had time to check your inbox.

Up and running in under a minute

Download the latest release and load it as an unpacked extension — no Chrome Web Store, no build steps, no configuration needed.

Download latest release ↗
  1. Download the latest release zip from GitHub Releases and unzip it
  2. Open chrome://extensions in Chrome and turn on Developer mode
  3. Click Load unpacked and choose the unzipped folder
  4. Click the 🐹 icon in your toolbar, enter your Gmail address, and save it
  5. Visit the Atlantis login page — your new button is already there
